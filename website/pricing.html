Billing FAQ

Questions people ask before they subscribe.

Short answers, no pricing maze.

Is Keystone Access one subscription for all three courses?

Yes. Keystone Access is one plan that unlocks Thai, Japanese, and Korean in the same account.

Can I cancel the monthly plan anytime?

Yes. Monthly access is designed to stay flexible. If you cancel, your paid access continues until the current billing period ends.

What is included in the yearly plan?

Everything in Keystone Access Monthly: the full lesson library, full practice flow, audio, bookmarks, progress, and vocabulary review.

Do I need a separate subscription for mobile and web?

No. The goal is one account that carries your access across the platform, even if billing is handled by different stores or payment systems.

Does free include enough to see how Keystone works?

Yes. Free is there to let you experience the lesson style, example sentences, audio, and practice before you decide to go deeper.

Will new lessons and course updates be included?

Yes. Keystone Access is meant to cover future lesson and course updates as the platform grows.
